### 1. Flexible family visitation and postoperative delirium in an orthopedic intensive care unit: A randomized trial examining surgical-type heterogeneity and cost outcomes.

- PMID: 42432993
- Journal: The Journal of international medical research (2026-Jul)
- Summary: Daytime flexible family visitation reduced postoperative delirium, sedative use, and hospitalization costs in older orthopedic ICU patients without raising infections or prolonging stay.
- 요약: 주간 유연 가족 면회는 고령 정형외과 중환자실 환자에서 감염 증가나 재원 연장 없이 수술 후 섬망, 진정제 사용, 입원 비용을 감소시켰다.
- Why it matters: Postoperative delirium affects >30–40% of older orthopedic ICU patients and drives poor recovery, cognitive decline, and mortality; this trial shows a simple, non-pharmacologic, low-cost policy change—liberalizing family visitation—can meaningfully cut delirium and costs.
- 중요성: 수술 후 섬망은 고령 정형외과 중환자실 환자의 30~40% 이상에서 발생하며 회복 저하, 인지 저하, 사망률 증가로 이어진다. 이 연구는 가족 면회 자유화라는 단순하고 비약물적이며 저비용의 정책 변화가 섬망과 비용을 의미 있게 줄일 수 있음을 보여준다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Single-center, parallel-group, 1:1 randomized controlled trial (CONSORT-compliant), with prespecified exploratory subgroup analyses by surgical type.
- Setting: Orthopedic ICU of Beijing Jishuitan Hospital Guizhou Hospital, China; flexible and restricted patients managed in two physically separated ICU areas ~5 m apart. Registered as ChiCTR2600117290 (public registry metadata); ethics approval 21 July 2023.
- Sample size: 420 patients randomized (210 per arm); 405 analyzed in complete-case primary analysis (200 flexible, 205 restricted). 15/420 (3.6%) excluded: flexible group 10/210 (unplanned discharge/transfer n=6, clinical deterioration n=4); restricted group 5/210 (incomplete data). Sample size planned a priori (PASS): 170/arm for 80% power, two-sided alpha 0.05 to detect 45% vs 30% delirium, inflated to 400 for 15% dropout.
- Eligibility: Inclusion: age >=60; admitted to orthopedic ICU within 24 h after orthopedic surgery; negative CAM-ICU preoperatively and at ICU admission; able to communicate for delirium assessment; expected ICU stay >=24 h; at least one fixed family member/caregiver willing to visit. Exclusion: moderate-to-severe dementia/severe psychiatric disorder; severe sensory/communication barriers; acute CNS disorders (TBI, stroke, status epilepticus); anticipated deep sedation or NMB >=24 h or delirium at admission; mandatory isolation; expected survival <24 h or comfort care.
- Intervention: Daytime flexible visitation: up to two family members per day with no predefined time limit; members could alternate; visits initiated at patient request or staff recommendation. For mechanically ventilated patients, bedside visitation permitted after extubation per infection control policy. Achieved median daily visiting time 5.0 h.
- Comparator: Routine restricted visitation: one visit per day from 16:00–17:00, no other visits and no overnight bedside stay. Achieved median daily visiting time 0.9 h. All other postoperative ICU care identical (VAS-guided analgesia titrated to pain <4, monitoring, respiratory/hemodynamic management, routine non-pharmacologic delirium prevention).
- Follow-up: Daily delirium assessment (CAM-ICU and ICDSC) until ICU discharge, transfer out of ICU, or in-hospital death; resource-use and cost data collected prospectively until hospital discharge or death. Exact overall follow-up duration not specified beyond hospitalization; median hospital LOS 16 days in both groups.
- Source basis: Open full text (PMC13354881), PubMed metadata, and Crossref metadata.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: 29.5% (59/200) with flexible vs 44.4% (91/205) with restricted (P=0.002)
  - ⓘ Flexible visitation cut delirium by about 15 absolute percentage points—roughly a third relative reduction.
  - 유연 면회는 섬망을 약 15%p 절대 감소시켰으며, 상대적으로 약 3분의 1 감소에 해당한다.
  - Stats rule: P<0.05 is conventionally statistically significant; here P=0.002 indicates the difference is unlikely due to chance, though this reflects statistical, not necessarily clinical, importance.
  - P<0.05는 통상 통계적으로 유의함을 의미하며, P=0.002는 이 차이가 우연에 의한 것일 가능성이 낮음을 나타낸다. 다만 이는 통계적 유의성이며 반드시 임상적 중요성을 의미하지는 않는다.
- Subgroup (exploratory): 36.5% with flexible vs 57.6% with restricted (P=0.026)
  - ⓘ Cervical spine patients showed the largest benefit, but this was a prespecified exploratory subgroup and is hypothesis-generating.
  - 경추 수술 환자에서 가장 큰 이익을 보였으나 이는 사전 지정 탐색적 하위군으로 가설 생성 수준이다.
  - Stats rule: Subgroup P values are exploratory; with multiple subgroups a single P<0.05 should be interpreted cautiously and not as confirmatory.
  - 하위군 P값은 탐색적이며, 여러 하위군을 검정할 때 단일 P<0.05는 확정적 근거가 아니라 신중히 해석해야 한다.
- Secondary: 32.0% with flexible vs 44.9% with restricted (P=0.008)
  - ⓘ Flexible visitation was associated with less sedative exposure, consistent with reduced agitation/anxiety.
  - 유연 면회는 진정제 노출 감소와 연관되어 초조/불안 감소와 일치한다.
  - Stats rule: P=0.008 (<0.05) is statistically significant; percentages compare the proportion of patients receiving sedatives in each arm.
  - P=0.008(<0.05)은 통계적으로 유의하다; 백분율은 각 군에서 진정제를 받은 환자 비율을 비교한다.
- Secondary (safety): 3.5% with flexible vs 3.9% with restricted (P=0.916)
  - ⓘ No signal of increased infection with liberalized visitation, an important safety reassurance.
  - 면회 자유화로 감염 증가 신호가 없어 중요한 안전성 근거가 된다.
  - Stats rule: P>=0.05 means no statistically significant difference was detected; note the study was powered for delirium, not infection, so it may be underpowered for rare safety events.
  - P>=0.05는 통계적으로 유의한 차이가 검출되지 않았음을 의미한다; 이 연구는 섬망을 위한 검정력으로 설계되어 드문 안전성 사건에는 검정력이 부족할 수 있다.
- Secondary: Median 16 days in both groups (P=0.367)
  - ⓘ Visitation policy did not shorten or prolong hospitalization length.
  - 면회 정책은 입원기간을 단축하거나 연장하지 않았다.
  - Stats rule: P>=0.05 indicates no statistically significant difference; medians are compared for non-normally distributed length-of-stay data.
  - P>=0.05는 통계적으로 유의한 차이가 없음을 나타낸다; 정규분포하지 않는 재원기간 자료는 중앙값으로 비교한다.
- Secondary: Lower with flexible visitation (exact values not reported in accessible sources) (P<0.001)
  - ⓘ Costs were significantly lower with flexible visitation, likely reflecting less delirium and sedative use; absolute cost figures are not available in the accessible sources.
  - 유연 면회에서 비용이 유의하게 낮았으며 이는 섬망 및 진정제 사용 감소를 반영할 가능성이 있다; 절대 비용 수치는 접근 가능한 자료에 없다.
  - Stats rule: P<0.001 is highly statistically significant, but without reported absolute cost values the magnitude and clinical/economic relevance cannot be judged.
  - P<0.001은 통계적으로 매우 유의하나, 절대 비용 값이 보고되지 않아 크기와 임상/경제적 의미는 판단할 수 없다.
- Takeaway: Liberalizing daytime family visitation is a low-cost, non-pharmacologic intervention that appears to reduce delirium and sedative use in older post-surgical orthopedic ICU patients without added infection risk; it is a reasonable, safe policy to adopt while awaiting multicenter confirmation.
- 임상 포인트: 주간 가족 면회 자유화는 저비용의 비약물적 중재로, 감염 위험 증가 없이 고령 수술 후 정형외과 중환자실 환자의 섬망과 진정제 사용을 줄이는 것으로 보인다; 다기관 확인을 기다리는 동안 채택할 만한 합리적이고 안전한 정책이다.
