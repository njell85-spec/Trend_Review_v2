### 1. Impact of Oxygen Targets on Sepsis Outcome: A Randomized Controlled Trial.

- PMID: 42337386
- Journal: Shock (Augusta, Ga.) (2026-Jul-01)
- Summary: In a single-center Chinese RCT of sepsis, a hyperoxygenation target (PaO2 >100-150 mmHg) lowered 28-day mortality versus a conservative target, but 90-day mortality did not differ.
- 요약: 중국 단일기관 패혈증 RCT에서 고산소화 목표(PaO2 >100-150 mmHg)가 보존적 목표보다 28일 사망률을 낮췄으나 90일 사망률에는 차이가 없었다.
- Why it matters: The optimal oxygenation target in sepsis is unresolved, and large mechanically ventilated ICU trials (UK-ROX, PILOT, ICONIC) have produced conflicting signals; this trial directly tests targets in a sepsis-specific population and provocatively favors higher PaO2, challenging the prevailing trend toward conservative oxygenation.
- 중요성: 패혈증에서 최적의 산소화 목표는 아직 정립되지 않았고, 대규모 인공호흡 ICU 시험들(UK-ROX, PILOT, ICONIC)은 상충된 결과를 보였다. 본 시험은 패혈증 특정 환자군에서 목표치를 직접 비교하여 보존적 산소화 경향에 도전하며 더 높은 PaO2를 지지하는 도발적 결과를 제시한다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Prospective, single-center, single-blinded, three-arm randomized controlled trial; computer-generated random numbers with sealed, opaque envelopes managed by personnel independent of the research team.
- Setting: Emergency Department of Beijing Hospital (National Center of Gerontology), Beijing, China; enrollment November 2022 to January 2025. Funded by the National High Level Hospital Clinical Research Funding (Crossref metadata).
- Sample size: 330 patients enrolled (108/111/111 to conservative/conventional/hyperoxygenation); 60 (18%) excluded after randomization; 270 analyzed who completed the study: conservative n=86, conventional n=93, hyperoxygenation n=91.
- Eligibility: Inclusion: age >=18 years; sepsis or septic shock by Sepsis-3 (2016) criteria; expected hospitalization >48 hours; informed consent. Exclusions listed in protocol included death within 48 h of enrollment, failure to achieve oxygenation target, diagnosis revised to exclude sepsis, and missing data (full exclusion list truncated in accessible excerpt).
- Intervention: Hyperoxygenation target group: PaO2 >100 and <150 mmHg. Oxygen titrated by adjusting inspired oxygen concentration 5%-10% per step (1-2 L/min via nasal cannula or other precise-adjustment devices), escalating to advanced oxygen therapy devices as needed; ABG-guided with SpO2 monitoring on enrollment day and days 1, 3, 7, 14.
- Comparator: Two comparator arms: conservative oxygenation target (60 mmHg <= PaO2 < 80 mmHg) and conventional oxygenation target (PaO2 80-100 mmHg), titrated by the same protocol. Device choice and parameters individualized to clinical condition.
- Follow-up: Telephone follow-up at 28 and 90 days; in-hospital monitoring on enrollment day and days 1, 3, 7, and 14.
- Source basis: PMC full text (PMC13313468) for design, eligibility, intervention/comparator targets, exclusions, and outcomes; PubMed metadata for affiliations; Crossref for publisher and funding.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: Conservative 40.7% (35/86), conventional 34.4% (32/93), hyperoxygenation 18.7% (17/91); omnibus P=0.005. Pairwise (Bonferroni) significant only conservative vs hyperoxygenation: chi2=10.132, P=0.001. (Omnibus chi-square P=0.005; pairwise chi2=10.132, P=0.001)
  - ⓘ Roughly half as many deaths in the hyperoxygenation group versus conservative; the conventional-vs-hyperoxygenation difference did not reach significance after correction.
  - 고산소화군 사망자가 보존적군의 약 절반 수준; 보정 후 통상 대 고산소화 차이는 유의하지 않았다.
  - Stats rule: Chi-square P<0.05 indicates the group proportions differ more than expected by chance; it does not give an effect size or direction, so read the actual mortality percentages to see which arm fared better.
  - 카이제곱 P<0.05는 군 간 비율이 우연 이상으로 다름을 의미하며 효과 크기나 방향은 주지 않으므로, 실제 사망률 백분율을 보고 어느 군이 더 나았는지 판단해야 한다.
- Survival: Survival time distributions differed significantly among the three groups: chi2=10.340, P=0.006. (Log-rank chi2=10.340, P=0.006)
  - ⓘ Time-to-death curves separated across arms, consistent with the lower early mortality in the hyperoxygenation group.
  - 사망까지의 곡선이 군 간 분리되어 고산소화군의 낮은 초기 사망률과 일치한다.
  - Stats rule: The log-rank test compares whole survival curves over time; P<0.05 means the curves differ. No hazard ratio or confidence interval was reported, so the magnitude of benefit cannot be quantified here.
  - Log-rank 검정은 시간에 따른 전체 생존곡선을 비교하며 P<0.05는 곡선이 다름을 의미한다. 위험비나 신뢰구간이 보고되지 않아 이득의 크기는 정량화할 수 없다.
- Secondary: Conservative 50.0%, conventional 41.9%, hyperoxygenation 36.3%; P=0.179 (not significant). (P=0.179)
  - ⓘ The numeric gradient still favored hyperoxygenation but the early mortality advantage was no longer statistically significant by 90 days.
  - 수치상 경향은 여전히 고산소화에 유리했으나 초기 사망 이득은 90일에는 통계적으로 유의하지 않았다.
  - Stats rule: P>=0.05 means the observed differences are compatible with chance; a nonsignificant result is not proof of no difference, especially with this sample size.
  - P>=0.05는 관찰된 차이가 우연과 양립함을 의미하며, 비유의 결과가 차이 없음을 증명하지는 않는다(특히 이 표본 크기에서).
- Secondary: No significant differences among the three groups for mechanical ventilation, intubation, vasopressor use, or ARDS incidence (exact values not provided in accessible excerpt). Among ventilated patients, FiO2 differed significantly on days 3 and 7. (Reported as not significant; exact P/values not available in accessible sources)
  - ⓘ Major organ-support interventions were similar across arms, so the mortality signal was not explained by differing ventilation or vasopressor exposure.
  - 주요 장기지지 중재가 군 간 유사하여, 사망률 신호가 환기나 승압제 노출 차이로 설명되지 않는다.
  - Stats rule: When a difference is reported as not significant, treat the arms as comparable for that endpoint; absence of exact values limits how confidently this can be judged.
  - 비유의로 보고된 경우 해당 지표에서 군을 유사한 것으로 보되, 정확한 수치 부재로 판단의 확신은 제한된다.
- Subgroup: Older (>=65 y): significant difference among groups (P=0.006); pairwise conservative vs hyperoxygenation chi2=10.072, P=0.002. Respiratory-source sepsis: P=0.003 among groups. (Older: P=0.006, pairwise chi2=10.072 P=0.002; respiratory sepsis P=0.003)
  - ⓘ The hyperoxygenation benefit was directionally consistent in elderly and respiratory-infection subgroups, but these are exploratory and underpowered.
  - 고산소화 이득은 고령 및 호흡기감염 하위군에서 방향적으로 일관됐으나, 탐색적이고 검정력이 부족하다.
  - Stats rule: Subgroup P values are hypothesis-generating; without interaction testing and with multiple comparisons, significant subgroup results can arise by chance.
  - 하위군 P값은 가설 생성용으로, 교호작용 검정 없이 다중 비교가 이루어지면 유의한 하위군 결과가 우연히 나올 수 있다.
- Takeaway: This hypothesis-generating single-center RCT suggests that in sepsis a higher PaO2 target (100-150 mmHg) may reduce early (28-day) mortality versus a conservative target, but with no 90-day benefit and notable methodologic limitations it should not yet change practice or override conservative-oxygenation guidance from larger ICU trials.
- 임상 포인트: 이 가설 생성적 단일기관 RCT는 패혈증에서 더 높은 PaO2 목표(100-150 mmHg)가 보존적 목표보다 초기(28일) 사망률을 낮출 수 있음을 시사하지만, 90일 이득이 없고 방법론적 한계가 뚜렷하여 아직 진료를 바꾸거나 대규모 ICU 시험의 보존적 산소화 권고를 대체해서는 안 된다.
