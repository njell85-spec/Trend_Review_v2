### 1. Severity of Chronic Kidney Disease and Outcomes After Admission to the Intensive Care Unit.

- PMID: 42348209
- Journal: JAMA network open (2026-Jun-01)
- Summary: In 531,090 Ontario ICU adults, increasing CKD severity was progressively associated with higher mortality and ICU kidney replacement therapy, except maintenance dialysis patients had lower mortality than non-dialysis stage 5 CKD.
- 요약: 온타리오 ICU 성인 531,090명에서 CKD 중증도가 높을수록 사망률과 ICU 신대체요법이 점진적으로 증가했으나, 유지투석 환자는 비투석 5기 CKD보다 사망률이 낮았다.
- Why it matters: CKD patients are overrepresented in the ICU (25% vs 12.5% in the general population), and this large population-based study quantifies how baseline kidney function—graded by KDIGO eGFR stage rather than diagnostic codes—independently tracks with ICU/hospital/90-day mortality, ICU KRT initiation, and long-term dialysis dependence, supporting bedside prognostication and goals-of-care discussions.
- 중요성: CKD 환자는 ICU에서 과대표집되며(일반 인구 12.5% 대비 25%), 이 대규모 인구기반 연구는 진단코드가 아닌 KDIGO eGFR 단계로 분류한 기저 신기능이 ICU·병원·90일 사망률, ICU 내 KRT 시작, 장기 투석 의존과 독립적으로 연관됨을 정량화하여 침상 예후평가와 치료목표 논의를 뒷받침한다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Population-based retrospective cohort study using linked administrative health databases at ICES, following RECORD and STROBE reporting guidelines.
- Setting: Ontario, Canada; all ICUs province-wide, index ICU admissions from November 1, 2008 to February 28, 2021; data linked at ICES (multiple administrative databases including CIHI DAD, NACRS, OHIP, CORR, OLIS, Critical Care Information System).
- Sample size: 531,090 adults analyzed (met eligibility from 848,202 Ontario ICU admissions in the accrual window). Distribution: no CKD 75%, stage 3a 12%, stage 3b 7%, stage 4 3%, non-dialysis-dependent stage 5 1%, maintenance dialysis 2%. A sensitivity analysis including patients without baseline creatinine (imputed eGFR 75) totaled n=824,951; an albuminuria subgroup had n=208,897 (39.3%).
- Eligibility: Ontario residents aged ≥18 years admitted to an ICU during the study period with a baseline outpatient serum creatinine measured 7–365 days before admission (first ICU admission used as index). Exclusions: prior kidney transplant, no baseline eGFR, ineligible for OHIP coverage.
- Intervention: Exposure was CKD severity by baseline outpatient eGFR (2021 CKD-EPI formula), classified by KDIGO stages: no CKD (≥60), stage 3a (45–59), stage 3b (30–44), stage 4 (15–29), non-dialysis-dependent stage 5 (<15), and maintenance dialysis. Albuminuria not in primary analysis due to limited availability.
- Comparator: Reference group was patients without CKD (baseline eGFR ≥60 mL/min/1.73 m2). Maintenance dialysis patients were excluded from KRT-in-ICU analyses.
- Follow-up: Outcomes captured during ICU stay, during hospital stay, and at 90 days after ICU admission; KRT dependence assessed at day 90. Loss to follow-up only through emigration (<0.5% annually).
- Source basis: Abstract, PMC open full text (PMC13306308), PubMed metadata, and Crossref metadata.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: Relative to no CKD: stage 3a AOR 1.14 (95% CI, 1.11–1.17); stage 3b AOR 1.38 (1.34–1.42); stage 4 AOR 1.95 (1.87–2.03); non-dialysis-dependent stage 5 AOR 2.32 (2.14–2.52); maintenance dialysis OR 1.92 (1.82–2.04). (AOR 1.14 / 1.38 / 1.95 / 2.32; dialysis OR 1.92, all 95% CIs excluding 1.0)
  - ⓘ Mortality odds rose stepwise with worsening CKD up to non-dialysis stage 5; maintenance dialysis patients had lower odds than non-dialysis stage 5 and comparable to stage 4.
  - 사망 위험은 비투석 5기까지 CKD 악화에 따라 단계적으로 증가; 유지투석 환자는 비투석 5기보다 낮고 4기와 유사했다.
  - Stats rule: OR/AOR of 1 means no difference; >1 means higher odds of death; a 95% CI not crossing 1 indicates conventional statistical significance.
  - OR/AOR 1은 차이 없음, 1보다 크면 사망 위험 증가; 95% CI가 1을 포함하지 않으면 통계적 유의성을 의미한다.
- Secondary: Relative to no CKD: stage 3a AOR 1.79 (1.68–1.90); stage 3b AOR 3.02 (2.83–3.22); stage 4 AOR 6.71 (6.23–7.22); non-dialysis-dependent stage 5 AOR 32.00 (29.07–35.22). (AOR 1.79 / 3.02 / 6.71 / 32.00, all 95% CIs excluding 1.0)
  - ⓘ Risk of needing ICU dialysis climbed steeply—about 32-fold for non-dialysis stage 5 versus patients without CKD.
  - ICU 투석 필요 위험은 가파르게 증가하여 비투석 5기는 CKD 없는 환자 대비 약 32배였다.
  - Stats rule: AOR of 1 means no difference; larger values mean greatly increased odds of ICU KRT; CIs well above 1 confirm significance.
  - AOR 1은 차이 없음; 값이 클수록 ICU KRT 위험이 크게 증가; CI가 1보다 충분히 크면 유의함을 확인한다.
- Secondary: Proportion KRT-dependent at day 90 increased by stage: no CKD 7.2%; stage 3a 14.2%; stage 3b 22.5%; stage 4 50.3%; previously non-dialysis-dependent stage 5 83.8%. (Descriptive proportions: 7.2% / 14.2% / 22.5% / 50.3% / 83.8%)
  - ⓘ Among survivors who started ICU dialysis, recovery off dialysis was far less likely with more severe baseline CKD—most stage 4 and nearly all prior stage 5 patients remained dialysis-dependent.
  - ICU 투석을 시작한 생존자 중 기저 CKD가 심할수록 투석 중단 가능성이 훨씬 낮아 4기 대부분과 이전 5기 거의 전부가 투석 의존 상태로 남았다.
  - Stats rule: These are descriptive percentages; higher values mean more patients remained dialysis-dependent. No ratio or p value is reported for this comparison.
  - 이는 기술적 백분율로, 값이 높을수록 더 많은 환자가 투석 의존 상태로 남았음을 의미한다. 이 비교에는 비율이나 p값이 보고되지 않았다.
- Secondary: Adjusted subdistribution HR for discharge (sHR<1 = longer stay): stage 3a 0.94 (0.93–0.95); stage 3b 0.87 (0.86–0.88); stage 4 0.77 (0.76–0.79); non-dialysis stage 5 0.65 (0.62–0.67); maintenance dialysis 0.71 (0.70–0.73). (Adjusted sHR 0.94 / 0.87 / 0.77 / 0.65 / 0.71, all 95% CIs below 1.0)
  - ⓘ More severe CKD meant longer hospital stays (lower discharge rate), with maintenance dialysis somewhat less prolonged than non-dialysis stage 5.
  - CKD가 심할수록 병원 재원기간이 길어졌으며(퇴원율 감소), 유지투석은 비투석 5기보다 다소 덜 연장되었다.
  - Stats rule: Because the event is discharge, sHR of 1 means no difference; sHR<1 means slower discharge (longer stay); CIs not crossing 1 indicate significance.
  - 사건이 퇴원이므로 sHR 1은 차이 없음; sHR<1이면 퇴원 지연(재원 연장); CI가 1을 포함하지 않으면 유의함을 의미한다.
- Takeaway: Use baseline outpatient eGFR/KDIGO stage as an independent prognostic marker in ICU patients: advancing CKD predicts higher mortality, much higher likelihood of needing dialysis in the ICU, and—especially in stage 4–5—a high chance of not recovering kidney function, which should frame realistic conversations with patients and families. Notably, maintenance dialysis patients are not the highest-risk group; their mortality resembles stage 4 CKD.
- 임상 포인트: ICU 환자에서 기저 외래 eGFR/KDIGO 단계를 독립적 예후 지표로 활용하라: CKD가 진행할수록 사망률이 높고 ICU에서 투석이 필요할 가능성이 훨씬 크며, 특히 4–5기에서는 신기능 회복 실패 가능성이 높아 환자·가족과의 현실적 대화의 틀이 되어야 한다. 주목할 점은 유지투석 환자가 최고위험군이 아니며 사망률이 4기 CKD와 유사하다는 것이다.
