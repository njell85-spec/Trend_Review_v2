### 1. [Effect of external diaphragmatic pacing on diaphragmatic function and weaning success rate in patients with acute exacerbations of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease undergoing invasive mechanical ventilation].

- PMID: 42427323
- Journal: Zhonghua wei zhong bing ji jiu yi xue (2026-Jun)
- Summary: In ventilated AECOPD patients, adding external diaphragmatic pacing to standard care raised weaning success to 93% vs 61% and lowered mortality.
- 요약: 침습적 기계환기를 받는 AECOPD 환자에서 표준치료에 체외 횡격막 페이싱을 추가하니 이탈 성공률이 61%에서 93%로 상승하고 사망률이 감소했다.
- Why it matters: Diaphragmatic dysfunction is a major driver of weaning failure in COPD, and this single-center RCT suggests a noninvasive, drug-free adjunct could meaningfully improve liberation from mechanical ventilation and survival.
- 중요성: 횡격막 기능장애는 COPD에서 이탈 실패의 주요 원인으로, 이 단일기관 RCT는 비침습적이고 약물이 필요 없는 보조요법이 기계환기 이탈과 생존을 의미 있게 개선할 수 있음을 시사한다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Prospective, single-center randomized controlled trial using block randomization into two parallel arms.
- Setting: Department of Critical Care Medicine, Hengshui People's Hospital, Hengshui, China, from November 1, 2022 to January 31, 2025.
- Sample size: 174 AECOPD patients on invasive mechanical ventilation were enrolled and randomized, 87 per group (control vs EDP). The abstract reports outcomes on all 87 per arm; separate ITT, per-protocol, and safety populations are not distinguished in the accessible sources.
- Eligibility: Patients with acute exacerbations of COPD (AECOPD) requiring invasive mechanical ventilation admitted to the ICU. MeSH indexing indicates adults spanning middle-aged and aged, both male and female. Detailed inclusion/exclusion criteria are not available in the accessible sources.
- Intervention: Conventional therapy plus external diaphragmatic pacing (EDP), a noninvasive technique that electrically stimulates the phrenic nerve/diaphragm via surface electrodes. Exact stimulation parameters, session frequency, duration per session, and total treatment duration are not available in the accessible sources.
- Comparator: Conventional (standard) therapy alone for ventilated AECOPD without external diaphragmatic pacing. Specific components of conventional therapy are not detailed in the accessible sources.
- Follow-up: Outcomes tracked through the ICU stay and included 28-day post-ICU discharge mortality and overall mortality; exact total follow-up window beyond this is not specified in the accessible sources.
- Source basis: Based on the English abstract and PubMed metadata (affiliations, MeSH, publication types). No PMCID or trial registry ID was available for open full-text or registry enrichment.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: EDP 93% (81/87) vs control 61% (53/87), P<0.05 (93% vs 61%, P<0.05)
  - ⓘ Nearly all EDP patients were successfully weaned versus about six in ten controls, a large absolute improvement of roughly 32 percentage points.
  - EDP군은 거의 모든 환자가 성공적으로 이탈한 반면 대조군은 약 10명 중 6명으로, 약 32%포인트의 큰 절대 개선을 보였다.
  - Stats rule: For proportions, a higher percentage with the intervention and P<0.05 indicates a statistically significant benefit; the absolute difference here (~32%) reflects clinical magnitude.
  - 비율에서 중재군의 백분율이 더 높고 P<0.05이면 통계적으로 유의한 이득을 의미하며, 여기서의 절대 차이(약 32%)는 임상적 크기를 반영한다.
- Secondary: DE (mm) 3.49±0.56 vs 2.82±0.47; DTF 35.72±4.59% vs 29.61±4.81%, all P<0.05 (DE 3.49 vs 2.82; DTF 35.72% vs 29.61%, P<0.05)
  - ⓘ EDP produced greater diaphragmatic excursion and thickening, indicating better restored diaphragm contractile function than standard care.
  - EDP는 더 큰 횡격막 이동거리와 두께 변화를 만들어 표준치료보다 횡격막 수축 기능이 더 잘 회복되었음을 시사한다.
  - Stats rule: These are mean ± SD values; higher DE and DTF mean better diaphragm function, and P<0.05 indicates the between-group difference is statistically significant.
  - 이들은 평균 ± 표준편차 값으로, DE와 DTF가 높을수록 횡격막 기능이 좋으며 P<0.05는 군 간 차이가 통계적으로 유의함을 의미한다.
- Secondary: Peak 24.86±7.12 vs 31.03±8.05; plateau 18.05±4.97 vs 23.08±6.21; resistance 28.96±4.85 vs 34.12±5.61; RR 20.13±2.46 vs 25.52±2.78; PaCO2 45.36±7.54 vs 49.81±8.05; PaO2 86.07±7.82 vs 82.18±8.14; PaO2/FiO2 253.57±51.96 vs 201.83±45.37, all P<0.05 (All comparisons P<0.05 favoring EDP)
  - ⓘ EDP lowered airway pressures, resistance, respiratory rate and PaCO2 while improving oxygenation, consistent with reduced respiratory load and better lung mechanics.
  - EDP는 기도압, 저항, 호흡수, PaCO2를 낮추고 산소화를 개선하여 호흡 부하 감소와 폐역학 향상과 일치한다.
  - Stats rule: Mean ± SD comparisons; direction of benefit depends on the parameter (lower pressures/resistance/RR/PaCO2 are better, higher PaO2/FiO2 is better), and P<0.05 marks statistical significance.
  - 평균 ± 표준편차 비교로, 이득 방향은 지표에 따라 다르며(압력/저항/호흡수/PaCO2는 낮을수록, PaO2/FiO2는 높을수록 좋음), P<0.05는 통계적 유의성을 나타낸다.
- Secondary: MV 5.84±1.72 vs 10.53±2.18 days; ICU LOS 9.74±2.15 vs 14.38±3.06 days, P<0.05 (MV ~4.7 days shorter; ICU LOS ~4.6 days shorter, P<0.05)
  - ⓘ EDP shortened ventilation by roughly 4.7 days and ICU stay by about 4.6 days, a substantial resource and patient benefit.
  - EDP는 환기 기간을 약 4.7일, 중환자실 재원을 약 4.6일 단축시켜 상당한 자원 및 환자 이득을 보였다.
  - Stats rule: Mean ± SD in days; a lower value with the intervention and P<0.05 indicates a statistically significant reduction.
  - 일수의 평균 ± 표준편차로, 중재군에서 값이 더 낮고 P<0.05이면 통계적으로 유의한 감소를 의미한다.
- Secondary: 28-day post-discharge mortality 7% (6/87) vs 20% (17/87); overall mortality 9% (8/87) vs 29% (25/87), P<0.05; no significant difference in reintubation rate or ICU mortality (28-day 7% vs 20%; overall 9% vs 29%, P<0.05; reintubation and ICU mortality NS)
  - ⓘ EDP was associated with lower post-discharge and overall mortality, though reintubation and in-ICU mortality did not differ significantly, suggesting benefit may accrue after ICU discharge.
  - EDP는 퇴실 후 및 전체 사망률 감소와 연관되었으나 재삽관과 중환자실 내 사망률은 유의한 차이가 없어, 이득이 주로 중환자실 퇴실 후 나타날 수 있음을 시사한다.
  - Stats rule: For proportions, lower percentage with the intervention and P<0.05 means statistically significant; 'NS' (not significant) means the difference could be due to chance.
  - 비율에서 중재군의 백분율이 더 낮고 P<0.05이면 통계적으로 유의하며, 'NS'(유의하지 않음)는 차이가 우연에 의한 것일 수 있음을 의미한다.
- Takeaway: External diaphragmatic pacing is a promising noninvasive adjunct to accelerate weaning in ventilated AECOPD, but the large single-center effect sizes need multicenter confirmation before routine adoption.
- 임상 포인트: 체외 횡격막 페이싱은 기계환기 AECOPD의 이탈을 촉진하는 유망한 비침습적 보조요법이지만, 단일기관의 큰 효과 크기는 일상 도입 전에 다기관 검증이 필요하다.
