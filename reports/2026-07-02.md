### 1. Direct- versus video laryngoscopy during suction assisted laryngoscopy and airway decontamination (SALAD): A randomized controlled simulation study.

- PMID: 42362759
- Journal: Scientific reports (2026-Jun-26)
- Summary: In a SALAD massive-contamination manikin model, hyperangulated videolaryngoscopy dramatically outperformed direct laryngoscopy for first-pass success and speed.
- 요약: SALAD 대량 오염 마네킹 모델에서 과각형 비디오후두경이 직접후두경보다 첫 시도 성공률과 속도에서 크게 우수했다.
- Why it matters: Aspiration and contaminated airways are common, high-stakes emergency scenarios where first-pass success is critical; this trial adds controlled evidence that hyperangulated VL may be preferable within the SALAD workflow, though only in simulation.
- 중요성: 흡인과 오염된 기도는 첫 시도 성공이 매우 중요한 흔하고 위험한 응급 상황이며, 이 연구는 SALAD 워크플로우에서 과각형 VL이 선호될 수 있다는 통제된 근거를 더한다(다만 시뮬레이션에 한함).

#### Study Details
- Design: 1:1 randomized controlled simulation (manikin) study, single-center, reported per CONSORT 2025; prospectively registered in the German Clinical Trials Register (DRKS00034683) on July 15, 2024. Allocation concealment via sequentially numbered sealed opaque envelopes prepared by an independent investigator; participants blinded to the study endpoint; outcomes collected by an external assessor not otherwise involved.
- Setting: Heidelberg University Hospital, Germany (~2600-bed university maximum-care hospital), during in-house and external continuing medical education events between July 15, 2024 and September 30, 2025.
- Sample size: 215 physicians assessed for eligibility; 15 excluded before randomization (12 declined, 3 incomplete baseline data); 200 randomized and analyzed, 100 in DL and 100 in hyperangulated VL. No separate ITT/per-protocol split reported. (Sample-size calculation targeted ~109/group; final set at 100/group for feasibility.)
- Eligibility: Inclusion: licensed physicians actively involved in clinical airway management with prior experience in both DL and VL and familiarity with the SALAD technique. Exclusion: refusal to participate or incomplete baseline data. Population was mostly anesthesiologists (86-87%), plus internal medicine, surgery, and other specialties.
- Intervention: Hyperangulated videolaryngoscopy using the C-MAC D-Blade (Karl Storz, Germany) within a standardized SALAD workflow. Shared equipment: DuCanto rigid suction catheter (SSCOR), size 7.5 AEROtube endotracheal tube with stylet, Ambu Spur II bag, Nasco SALAD simulator with 750 mL of thickened, dyed simulated vomit delivered by manual pump; airway, tube and stylet lubricated.
- Comparator: Direct laryngoscopy using a HEINE Optotechnik Standard LED laryngoscope with a Macintosh blade size 3 (Gilching, Germany) within the same standardized SALAD workflow and identical shared equipment.
- Follow-up: Outcomes captured during a single simulated intubation attempt per participant (first-pass success, time to successful intubation, time to first ventilation); no longitudinal follow-up applicable.
- Source basis: Based on the article abstract, open PMC full text (PMC13309530) including Methods/Results and Tables 1-2, and Crossref/PubMed metadata.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: VL 94.0% (94/100) vs DL 58.0% (58/100); risk difference 36% (95% CI 25-47); OR 11.34 (95% CI 4.54-28.35); p<0.001 (OR 11.34, 95% CI 4.54-28.35; risk difference 36% (95% CI 25-47); p<0.001)
  - ⓘ Nearly all VL attempts succeeded first-pass versus roughly six in ten with DL, a large absolute and relative advantage for VL.
  - VL은 거의 모든 시도가 첫 시도에 성공한 반면 DL은 약 열 중 여섯으로, VL이 절대적·상대적으로 큰 이점을 보였다.
  - Stats rule: OR of 1 means no difference; >1 favors more first-pass success with VL, and the 95% CI well above 1 indicates statistical significance. Risk difference of 0 means no absolute difference; 36% with a CI excluding 0 confirms a significant absolute benefit.
  - OR 1은 차이 없음을 의미하며, 1보다 크면 VL에서 첫 시도 성공이 더 많음을 뜻하고 95% CI가 1을 크게 상회하므로 통계적으로 유의하다. 위험차 0은 절대 차이 없음을 의미하며, CI가 0을 배제한 36%는 유의한 절대 이득을 확인한다.
- Secondary: Median 34 s (IQR 29-40) VL vs 41 s (IQR 35-48) DL; mean 36.2 (SD 13.3) vs 44.1 (SD 14.9); mean difference -7.9 s (95% CI -11.87 to -3.99); p<0.001 (Mann-Whitney U; mean difference -7.9 s (95% CI -11.87 to -3.99); p<0.001)
  - ⓘ VL shaved roughly 8 seconds off intubation time, a modest but statistically clear speed advantage.
  - VL은 삽관 시간을 약 8초 단축해 크지 않지만 통계적으로 명확한 속도 이점을 보였다.
  - Stats rule: For a mean difference, 0 means no difference; a negative value favors faster intubation with VL, and a 95% CI excluding 0 (here -11.87 to -3.99) indicates statistical significance. p<0.05 is conventionally significant.
  - 평균차에서 0은 차이 없음을 의미하며, 음수는 VL에서 삽관이 더 빠름을 뜻하고 95% CI가 0을 배제하면(여기 -11.87~-3.99) 통계적으로 유의하다. p<0.05는 관례적으로 유의하다.
- Secondary: Median 40 s (IQR 34-44) VL vs 45 s (IQR 40-53) DL; p<0.001 (Mann-Whitney U; p<0.001)
  - ⓘ Patients (manikins) were ventilated several seconds sooner with VL, consistent with the faster intubation.
  - VL에서 환기가 수 초 더 빨리 이루어져 더 빠른 삽관과 일치했다.
  - Stats rule: Medians with IQRs describe the middle value and spread; p<0.05 (here p<0.001) indicates the between-group difference is statistically significant, though a few seconds may be of modest clinical magnitude.
  - 중앙값과 IQR은 중간값과 분포를 나타내며, p<0.05(여기 p<0.001)는 군간 차이가 통계적으로 유의함을 뜻하나 수 초 차이는 임상적으로 크지 않을 수 있다.
- Adjusted analysis: Adjusted OR 13.42 (95% CI 5.18-34.78); p<0.001; other covariates not significantly associated (Adjusted OR 13.42, 95% CI 5.18-34.78; p<0.001)
  - ⓘ The VL advantage held after accounting for operator factors, indicating the device rather than experience or specialty drove success.
  - 시술자 요인을 보정한 후에도 VL의 이점이 유지되어, 경력이나 전공이 아니라 기기가 성공을 이끌었음을 시사한다.
  - Stats rule: Adjusted OR isolates the device effect from measured confounders; OR 1 means no association, and a CI entirely above 1 (5.18-34.78) confirms a significant independent benefit.
  - 보정 OR은 측정된 교란요인으로부터 기기 효과를 분리한다; OR 1은 연관 없음을 의미하며, CI 전체가 1을 상회하면(5.18-34.78) 유의한 독립적 이득을 확인한다.
- Takeaway: In a heavily contaminated-airway SALAD scenario, hyperangulated VL yielded far higher first-pass success and faster intubation/ventilation than DL in a manikin; consider prioritizing hyperangulated VL for SALAD, recognizing this is simulation-only evidence pending clinical confirmation.
- 임상 포인트: 심하게 오염된 기도 SALAD 상황에서 과각형 VL은 마네킹에서 DL보다 훨씬 높은 첫 시도 성공률과 빠른 삽관/환기를 보였다; SALAD 시 과각형 VL 우선 사용을 고려하되, 이는 임상 확인이 필요한 시뮬레이션 근거임을 유념한다.
