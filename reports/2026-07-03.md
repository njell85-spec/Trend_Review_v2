### 1. Alpha2 agonists for sedation to produce better outcomes for adults with critical illness: a synopsis of the A2B RCT with cost-effectiveness and process evaluation.

- PMID: 42384077
- Journal: Health technology assessment (Winchester, England) (2026-Jun)
- Summary: In mechanically ventilated ICU adults, neither dexmedetomidine- nor clonidine-based sedation improved time to extubation, mortality, or cost-effectiveness versus propofol.
- 요약: 기계환기 중인 성인 중환자에서 덱스메데토미딘 또는 클로니딘 기반 진정은 발관까지 시간, 사망률, 비용효과성에서 프로포폴보다 우수하지 않았다.
- Why it matters: Sedation choice is a daily ICU decision; this large pragmatic UK trial directly compares alpha2 agonists against the propofol-based standard of care and challenges the assumption that dexmedetomidine or clonidine speed liberation from ventilation, while highlighting more agitation and bradycardia.
- 중요성: 진정제 선택은 매일 이루어지는 중환자실 결정이며, 이 대규모 실용적 영국 시험은 알파2 작용제를 프로포폴 기반 표준치료와 직접 비교하여 덱스메데토미딘이나 클로니딘이 인공호흡기 이탈을 앞당긴다는 가정에 의문을 제기하는 동시에 더 많은 초조(agitation)와 서맥을 보여준다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Pragmatic, open-label, three-arm randomized controlled trial (A2B trial) with embedded process and health-economic (cost-effectiveness) evaluation.
- Setting: Forty-one intensive care units in the UK; recruitment December 2018 to October 2023, spanning the COVID-19 pandemic which affected staffing and delivery.
- Sample size: 1437 adults randomized/analyzed; analysis population by arm: propofol N=471, dexmedetomidine N=457, clonidine-based N=476.
- Eligibility: Adults within 48 hours of starting mechanical ventilation who were expected to require >=48 hours of mechanical ventilation. Median time from intubation to randomisation was 21.0 hours (Q1, Q3: 13.2, 31.3).
- Intervention: Two intervention arms: dexmedetomidine-based and clonidine-based sedation. Bedside algorithms targeted a RASS of -2 to +1 (unless deeper sedation clinically requested) and supported alpha2-agonist up-titration with propofol down-titration, then sedation primarily with the allocated alpha2 agonist; supplemental propofol was permitted if required. Exact doses, routes, and titration ranges are not available in the accessible sources.
- Comparator: Propofol-based sedation (usual care) plus an opioid analgesic, using the same RASS -2 to +1 target and bedside sedation algorithm.
- Follow-up: Clinical outcomes assessed through 180 days for mortality; long-term outcomes included ICU experience, anxiety/depression/PTSD, cognitive function, and health-related quality of life. Exact long-term assessment timepoints are not detailed in the accessible sources.
- Source basis: Abstract plus PubMed and Crossref metadata. This HTA monograph is a synopsis of the A2B RCT primary paper in JAMA 2025;334:32-45. No PMCID for open full-text; no ClinicalTrials.gov ID in the PubMed record. Funded by NIHR HTA award 16/93/01.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: Dexmedetomidine vs propofol subdistribution HR 1.09 (95% CI 0.96-1.25; p=0.20); clonidine vs propofol 1.05 (0.95-1.17; p=0.34). Median hours to extubation: propofol 162 (136-170), dexmedetomidine 136 (117-150), clonidine 146 (124-168). (sHR 1.09 (95% CI 0.96-1.25; p=0.20); sHR 1.05 (0.95-1.17; p=0.34))
  - ⓘ No statistically significant difference; although median times were numerically shorter with alpha2 agonists, the confidence intervals cross 1 and p values exceed 0.05.
  - 통계적으로 유의한 차이 없음; 알파2 작용제에서 중앙값 시간이 수치상 짧았으나 신뢰구간이 1을 포함하고 p값이 0.05를 초과함.
  - Stats rule: For a subdistribution hazard ratio, HR>1 favors faster extubation with the alpha2 agonist; if the 95% CI crosses 1 and p>=0.05, a significant difference is not established.
  - 하위분포 위험비에서 HR>1은 알파2 작용제로 더 빠른 발관을 의미; 95% CI가 1을 포함하고 p>=0.05이면 유의한 차이가 입증되지 않음.
- Secondary: Dexmedetomidine vs propofol HR 0.98 (95% CI 0.77-1.24); clonidine vs propofol HR 1.04 (0.82-1.31). (HR 0.98 (0.77-1.24); HR 1.04 (0.82-1.31))
  - ⓘ Mortality was similar across arms; both confidence intervals include 1, indicating no detectable survival difference.
  - 사망률은 군 간 유사; 두 신뢰구간 모두 1을 포함하여 생존 차이가 감지되지 않음.
  - Stats rule: HR=1 means no difference in mortality risk; a 95% CI crossing 1 means no statistically significant difference.
  - HR=1은 사망 위험 차이 없음을 의미; 95% CI가 1을 포함하면 통계적으로 유의한 차이 없음.
- Secondary (safety): Dexmedetomidine vs propofol RR 1.54 (95% CI 1.21-1.97); clonidine vs propofol RR 1.55 (1.22-1.97). (RR 1.54 (1.21-1.97); RR 1.55 (1.22-1.97))
  - ⓘ Both alpha2 agonists produced roughly 50% more agitation than propofol, and the CIs exclude 1, so the increase is statistically significant.
  - 두 알파2 작용제 모두 프로포폴보다 약 50% 더 많은 초조를 유발했고 CI가 1을 제외하여 통계적으로 유의한 증가임.
  - Stats rule: RR>1 means more agitation with the alpha2 agonist; a 95% CI entirely above 1 indicates a statistically significant increase.
  - RR>1은 알파2 작용제에서 초조가 더 많음을 의미; 95% CI가 전부 1 위에 있으면 통계적으로 유의한 증가.
- Secondary (safety): Dexmedetomidine vs propofol rate ratio 1.62 (95% CI 1.36-1.93); clonidine vs propofol 1.58 (1.33-1.88). (rate ratio 1.62 (1.36-1.93); 1.58 (1.33-1.88))
  - ⓘ Severe bradycardia was significantly more frequent with both alpha2 agonists, consistent with their known cardiovascular effects.
  - 두 알파2 작용제 모두에서 심한 서맥이 유의하게 더 빈번했으며 이는 알려진 심혈관 효과와 일치함.
  - Stats rule: A rate ratio >1 means more bradycardia events with the alpha2 agonist; a 95% CI above 1 indicates statistical significance.
  - 발생률비 >1은 알파2 작용제에서 서맥 사건이 더 많음을 의미; 95% CI가 1 위에 있으면 통계적 유의성을 나타냄.
- Secondary: Delirium rates were similar across arms; there was no difference in cost-effectiveness between the groups. (No between-group difference reported (exact estimates not in accessible sources))
  - ⓘ Alpha2 agonists did not reduce delirium and offered no economic advantage over propofol.
  - 알파2 작용제는 섬망을 줄이지 않았고 프로포폴 대비 경제적 이점도 없었음.
  - Stats rule: When no difference is reported, effect estimates are near the null and confidence intervals include the no-difference value.
  - 차이가 없다고 보고되면 효과 추정치가 무효값 근처에 있고 신뢰구간이 무차이 값을 포함함.
- Takeaway: Routinely switching to dexmedetomidine- or clonidine-based sedation is not justified for faster ventilator liberation or better outcomes; propofol remains a reasonable default, and alpha2 agonists should be used selectively while watching for agitation and bradycardia.
