### 1. Interpreting protein dose trials in critical illness: a guide for the bedside clinician.

- PMID: 42365349
- Journal: Critical care (London, England) (2026-Jun-27)
- Summary: This narrative review synthesizes recent RCT evidence and suggests starting ICU protein low and increasing gradually to a maximum of 1.2 g/kg/day.
- 요약: 이 종설은 최근 무작위 대조시험 근거를 종합하여 중환자 단백질 공급을 낮게 시작해 점진적으로 최대 1.2 g/kg/day까지 늘릴 것을 제안한다.
- Why it matters: Critical care nutrition guidelines have long recommended high protein doses, but three large recent trials found no benefit and possible harm, so this bedside guide helps clinicians reconcile older recommendations with new data.
