### 1. Assessing Risk of Early ICU Admission or Death at Emergency Department Triage: Clinical Judgment Versus Early Warning Scores.

- PMID: 42461154
- Journal: Academic emergency medicine : official journal of the Society for Academic Emergency Medicine (2026-Jul)
- Summary: In a large retrospective ED cohort, triage clinical judgment predicted 24-h ICU admission or death as well as the best Early Warning Score and better than the others.
- 요약: 대규모 후향적 응급실 코호트에서 분류(triage) 임상 판단은 24시간 내 중환자실 입실 또는 사망 예측에서 최고 성능의 조기경보점수와 대등했고 나머지 점수보다는 우수했다.
- Why it matters: Early Warning Scores are increasingly promoted or mandated in EDs, but this large study suggests structured scores do not clearly outperform experienced triage judgment for early adverse outcomes, cautioning against assuming EWS should replace clinician gestalt.
- 중요성: 조기경보점수는 응급실에서 점점 더 권장되거나 의무화되고 있으나, 이 대규모 연구는 구조화된 점수가 조기 악화 결과 예측에서 숙련된 분류 판단을 명확히 능가하지 못함을 시사하며 EWS가 임상적 직관을 대체해야 한다는 가정에 신중을 요한다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Retrospective, single-center observational comparative study (registered publication types: Observational Study, Comparative Study). Predictive performance of triage clinical judgment was compared with five computed EWS using discrimination (AUC), calibration, decision curve analysis, and reclassification metrics.
- Setting: Tertiary-care academic center. Author affiliations indicate Fondazione Policlinico Universitario A. Gemelli IRCCS and Universita Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, Rome, Italy; ED presentations spanned 2015-2024.
- Sample size: 361,927 adult ED presentations were included over 2015-2024. The abstract does not separately report distinct enrolled versus analyzed subpopulations or per-arm denominators (this is a prognostic model comparison, not randomized arms).
- Eligibility: Adult ED presentations at the tertiary-care academic center. Exact inclusion/exclusion criteria and handling of missing vital signs are not available in the accessible sources.
- Intervention: No treatment intervention; the 'index test' was clinical judgment operationalized as the triage category assigned at initial evaluation. No dosing/route applies.
- Comparator: Five Early Warning Scores computed from vital signs recorded at ED presentation: NEWS, NEWS2, MEWS, REMS, and ViEWS. Exact vital-sign components and thresholds are per each published score; specific cutoffs used are not detailed in accessible sources.
- Follow-up: Outcomes assessed within 24 hours of ED presentation (24-h mortality or ICU admission).

#### Outcomes
- Primary: ViEWS AUC 0.875 (highest) and clinical judgment AUC 0.872, with no significant difference between them; other EWS (NEWS, NEWS2, MEWS, REMS) had significantly lower AUCs. Event rate 1.17%. (AUC 0.875 (ViEWS) vs 0.872 (clinical judgment), no significant difference; other EWS significantly lower.)
  - ⓘ Clinician triage judgment discriminated early deterioration essentially as well as the top-performing score and better than the rest.
  - 임상의 분류 판단은 조기 악화를 최고 성능 점수와 사실상 대등하게, 나머지보다는 더 잘 판별했다.
  - Stats rule: AUC ranges 0.5 (no better than chance) to 1.0 (perfect); ~0.87 indicates good discrimination, and overlapping/non-significant differences mean models perform comparably.
  - AUC는 0.5(우연 수준)에서 1.0(완벽)까지의 범위이며, 약 0.87은 좋은 판별력을 의미하고, 겹치거나 유의하지 않은 차이는 모델들이 유사하게 작동함을 뜻한다.
- Secondary: Clinical judgment maintained higher specificity at higher-risk thresholds while preserving adequate sensitivity; decision curve analysis showed comparable or greater net benefit for clinical judgment than all tested EWS; reclassification metrics showed no improvement with EWS over clinical judgment. Exact values not available in accessible sources. (Decision curve analysis (net benefit) and reclassification metrics; no numeric estimates reported in accessible sources.)
  - ⓘ Across clinically relevant decision thresholds, adopting an EWS instead of triage judgment did not add value and sometimes offered less net benefit.
  - 임상적으로 관련된 결정 임계값 전반에서 분류 판단 대신 EWS를 채택하는 것은 가치를 더하지 않았고 때로는 더 적은 순이익을 제공했다.
  - Stats rule: Decision curve net benefit: higher curves across threshold probabilities indicate a more clinically useful model; reclassification (e.g., NRI/IDI) near zero means no meaningful improvement in risk classification.
  - 결정곡선 순이익: 임계 확률 전반에서 더 높은 곡선은 임상적으로 더 유용한 모델을 의미하며, 재분류 지표(예: NRI/IDI)가 0에 가까우면 위험 분류에서 의미 있는 개선이 없음을 뜻한다.
- Takeaway: Experienced triage judgment remains a strong predictor of early deterioration and should not be discarded in favor of EWS; scores may best serve as a complementary safety net rather than a replacement for clinician assessment.
