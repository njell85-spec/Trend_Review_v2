### 1. Respiratory support in paediatric patients with bronchiolitis: a systematic review and meta-analysis.

- PMID: 42386310
- Journal: European respiratory review : an official journal of the European Respiratory Society (2026-Jul)
- Summary: In infants with bronchiolitis, HFNC reduced treatment failure, PICU length of stay, and oxygen duration versus LFNC, but showed no advantage over CPAP.
- 요약: 세기관지염 영아에서 HFNC는 LFNC보다 치료 실패, PICU 재원기간, 산소 사용기간을 줄였으나 CPAP 대비 이점은 없었다.
- Why it matters: Bronchiolitis is a leading cause of infant hospital and PICU admission, and clinicians frequently must choose between standard low-flow oxygen, HFNC, and CPAP for escalating respiratory support; this RCT-only meta-analysis helps clarify where HFNC actually adds value and where it does not.
- 중요성: 세기관지염은 영아 입원 및 PICU 입원의 주요 원인이며, 임상의는 호흡지원을 올릴 때 표준 저유량 산소, HFNC, CPAP 중에서 선택해야 한다. RCT만을 대상으로 한 이 메타분석은 HFNC가 실제로 어디에서 이점이 있고 없는지 명확히 하는 데 도움이 된다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Systematic review and meta-analysis of randomised controlled trials, following PRISMA guidelines, using random-effects (DerSimonian-Laird) pairwise meta-analysis; registered on PROSPERO (CRD42024504872). Risk of bias assessed with the Cochrane RoB-2 tool. Analysis performed in Review Manager v5.0; p<0.05 considered significant, I-squared used for heterogeneity (p-value cut-off 0.1).
- Setting: Multiple countries and care settings across included RCTs: 11 studies in emergency department/paediatric ward settings and 7 in PICUs. Author affiliations span the UK, Greece, Cyprus, and Ireland (per public metadata). Literature search covered PubMed, Scopus, Cochrane Library, Embase, CINAHL, WHO ICTRP, and ClinicalTrials.gov up to 21 January 2024.
- Sample size: 18 RCTs with 4094 paediatric patients total. HFNC vs LFNC: 12 studies, 3273 patients (HFNC n=1633, LFNC n=1640). HFNC vs CPAP: 6 studies, 821 patients (HFNC n=415, CPAP n=406). Individual outcome analyses used varying study/patient subsets (e.g., mechanical ventilation HFNC vs LFNC: 11 studies, 3071 patients; length of PICU stay HFNC vs LFNC: 3 studies, 2306 patients).
- Eligibility: Randomised controlled studies including paediatric patients aged ≤24 months with bronchiolitis comparing HFNC versus LFNC (standard oxygen therapy) or versus CPAP. Articles in English, French, and Spanish were considered. One quasi-randomised study was included; three were pilot RCTs and one was terminated early during enrolment. One study published in 2011, the rest between 2017 and 2023.
- Intervention: High-flow nasal cannula (HFNC) as initial or escalation respiratory support. LFNC comparators varied across studies (standard nasal cannula, reservoir/non-rebreather mask, head box oxygen, low-flow OxyMask). Escalation and weaning strategies were reported in 11 studies each and varied between studies. Specific HFNC flow rates, FiO2, and durations are not aggregated in the accessible sources.
- Comparator: Two comparators: (1) LFNC / standard oxygen therapy; (2) CPAP. In the HFNC vs CPAP group, three studies used CPAP, two used nasal CPAP, and one used CPAP/noninvasive positive-pressure ventilation. Specific CPAP pressures are not available in the accessible sources.
- Follow-up: Outcomes were in-hospital measures (need for mechanical ventilation, treatment failure, PICU admission, length of hospital and PICU stay, days of total oxygen therapy, adverse events, in-hospital and PICU mortality). No longer-term follow-up beyond the hospitalization episode is described in the accessible sources.
- Source basis: Based on the PubMed abstract, Crossref metadata, and the open PMC full text (PMC13320613), including detailed per-outcome study/patient counts and adverse event tabulations.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: No significant reduction with HFNC (11 studies, 3071 patients). (RR 0.69, 95% CI 0.33–1.44; I²=42%; p=0.32)
  - ⓘ HFNC did not lower the chance of needing mechanical ventilation compared with LFNC; the wide CI crossing 1 reflects imprecision.
  - HFNC는 LFNC 대비 기계환기 필요 가능성을 낮추지 못했으며, 1을 지나는 넓은 신뢰구간은 정밀도가 낮음을 반영함.
  - Stats rule: RR of 1 means no difference; below 1 favours HFNC; because the 95% CI crosses 1 and p≥0.05, the result is not statistically significant.
  - RR 1은 차이 없음, 1 미만은 HFNC에 유리; 95% CI가 1을 지나고 p≥0.05이므로 통계적으로 유의하지 않음.
- Primary: No difference between HFNC and CPAP (5 studies, 771 patients). (RR 0.90, 95% CI 0.65–1.24; I²=0%; p=0.52)
  - ⓘ HFNC and CPAP performed similarly for avoiding mechanical ventilation, with no heterogeneity between studies.
  - HFNC와 CPAP는 기계환기 회피 측면에서 유사했으며 연구 간 이질성이 없었음.
  - Stats rule: RR near 1 with a CI crossing 1 indicates no meaningful difference; I²=0% means results were consistent across studies.
  - RR이 1에 가깝고 CI가 1을 지나면 의미 있는 차이 없음; I²=0%는 연구 간 결과가 일관됨을 의미.
- Primary: Significantly less treatment failure with HFNC (11 studies, 3165 patients). (RR 0.44, 95% CI 0.23–0.87; I²=92%; p=0.02)
  - ⓘ HFNC roughly halved treatment failure vs LFNC, but very high heterogeneity (I²=92%) and authors' note on doubtful clinical significance temper confidence.
  - HFNC는 LFNC 대비 치료 실패를 약 절반으로 줄였으나, 매우 높은 이질성(I²=92%)과 임상적 의의가 의심스럽다는 저자 지적으로 신뢰도는 제한됨.
  - Stats rule: RR 0.44 means ~56% relative reduction; CI entirely below 1 with p<0.05 is statistically significant, but I²=92% signals major inconsistency across studies.
  - RR 0.44는 약 56% 상대 감소; CI 전체가 1 미만이고 p<0.05로 통계적으로 유의하나, I²=92%는 연구 간 큰 불일치를 나타냄.
- Primary: No significant difference between HFNC and CPAP (6 studies, 827 patients). (RR 1.28, 95% CI 0.90–1.80; I²=57%; p=0.17)
  - ⓘ Point estimate numerically favours CPAP but the CI crosses 1; HFNC was not associated with increased treatment failure vs CPAP.
  - 점추정치는 수치상 CPAP에 유리하나 CI가 1을 지남; HFNC가 CPAP 대비 치료 실패를 증가시키지는 않았음.
  - Stats rule: RR above 1 suggests more failures with HFNC, but because the 95% CI crosses 1 and p≥0.05, no significant difference is established.
  - RR이 1보다 크면 HFNC에서 실패가 더 많음을 시사하나, 95% CI가 1을 지나고 p≥0.05이므로 유의한 차이는 확립되지 않음.
- Secondary: Significant reduction with HFNC (3 studies, 2306 patients). (MD −0.40, 95% CI −0.78 to −0.02; I²=85%; p=0.04)
  - ⓘ HFNC shortened PICU stay by about 0.4 days vs LFNC—statistically significant but small in magnitude and with high heterogeneity.
  - HFNC는 LFNC 대비 PICU 재원을 약 0.4일 단축—통계적으로 유의하나 크기가 작고 이질성이 높음.
  - Stats rule: Mean difference of 0 means no difference; the entire CI below 0 with p<0.05 indicates a statistically significant shorter stay, though the ~0.4 day effect is clinically modest.
  - 평균차 0은 차이 없음; CI 전체가 0 미만이고 p<0.05이면 통계적으로 유의한 재원 단축이나, 약 0.4일 효과는 임상적으로 미미함.
- Secondary: Significant reduction with HFNC (9 studies, 2370 patients). Length of PICU stay vs CPAP (MD −0.24, 95% CI −0.91 to 0.43; p=0.47) and length of hospital stay vs LFNC (MD −0.31, 95% CI −0.72 to 0.09; p=0.13) showed no difference. (MD −0.37, 95% CI −0.63 to −0.12; I²=81%; p=0.004)
  - ⓘ HFNC modestly shortened total oxygen therapy duration vs LFNC; no significant differences in hospital stay or in any endpoint vs CPAP. No deaths in HFNC group across studies.
  - HFNC는 LFNC 대비 총 산소요법 기간을 다소 단축; 병원 재원이나 CPAP 대비 모든 지표에서 유의한 차이 없음. 여러 연구에서 HFNC군 사망 없음.
  - Stats rule: MD below 0 with a CI not crossing 0 and p<0.05 is significant; when the CI crosses 0 (as for hospital stay and CPAP comparisons), no significant difference is shown.
  - MD가 0 미만이고 CI가 0을 지나지 않으며 p<0.05이면 유의; CI가 0을 지나면(병원 재원 및 CPAP 비교처럼) 유의한 차이 없음.
- Takeaway: Use HFNC preferentially over LFNC when escalation is needed in bronchiolitis, expecting fewer treatment failures and modestly shorter PICU/oxygen days, but do not expect it to prevent intubation or to outperform CPAP—choose between HFNC and CPAP based on local resources, tolerability, and expertise.
- 임상 포인트: 세기관지염에서 단계 상승이 필요할 때 LFNC보다 HFNC를 우선 사용하면 치료 실패가 줄고 PICU/산소 사용일이 다소 단축될 수 있으나, 삽관 예방이나 CPAP 대비 우월성은 기대하지 말고, HFNC와 CPAP 중 선택은 지역 자원·내약성·숙련도에 따라 결정하라.
