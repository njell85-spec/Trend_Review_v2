### 1. Opioid Use and Pain Resolution for Acute Pain Among Opioid-Naive Patients.

- PMID: 42412431
- Journal: JAMA network open (2026-Jul-01)
- Summary: In opioid-naive US patients offered opioids for acute pain, use was generally low-dose and short (median 7 days), with most reaching pain resolution within ~3 weeks but longer for surgical and low back pain.
- 요약: 오피오이드 미경험 미국 환자에서 급성 통증에 오피오이드를 제안받았을 때 사용은 대체로 저용량·단기간(중앙값 7일)이었고, 대부분 약 3주 내 통증이 해소되었으나 수술 및 요통은 더 오래 걸렸다.
- Why it matters: It provides real-world, prospectively collected pain trajectory and opioid utilization data across diverse acute pain sources, informing how well current short-course, multimodal prescribing guidelines fit the actual patients seen in EDs and other acute care settings.
- 중요성: 다양한 급성 통증 원인에 걸쳐 전향적으로 수집한 실제 통증 경과와 오피오이드 사용 자료를 제공하여, 현재의 단기·다중모드 처방 지침이 응급실 등 급성 진료 환자에게 얼마나 부합하는지를 알려준다.

#### Study Details
- Design: Prospective observational cohort study (descriptive; no randomization or comparator arm). Metadata carries a 'Practice Guideline' study-type signal, but the article itself is a cohort study; PubMed publication type is Journal Article.
- Setting: 5 US health systems, September 2020 to March 2023; recruitment across emergency departments, primary/urgent care clinics, dental practices, and inpatient settings (after cesarean delivery or knee replacement). Affiliated centers per metadata include Mayo Clinic (Rochester, MN), University of Alabama at Birmingham, Yale, Weill Cornell, and Cedars-Sinai; analysis conducted April 2023 through February 2026.
- Sample size: 1708 enrolled patients (median age 38 years, IQR 28-52). Denominators vary by outcome: 1502 reported pain level at least once; 1189 (69.6%) reported any opioid use; 1482 (86.8%) completed at least 1 survey in the first 2 weeks; dose reported by 619 opioid users; leftover-opioid data from 982 respondents.
- Eligibility: Opioid-naive adults aged >=18 years at all sites who were offered an opioid prescription for acute pain; also adolescents aged 15-17 undergoing impacted molar extraction at 1 site. Pain sources included dental (17.7%), trauma/injury (17.7%), obstetric (10.3%), musculoskeletal (7.7%), and low back (5.9%).
- Intervention: Exposure was being offered a prescription for an opioid analgesic. Among users reporting dose, daily doses were low (median 10 MME/day, IQR 5-15). Specific opioid agents, routes, and prescribed quantities/durations are not detailed in the accessible sources; use was self-reported. Nonopioid use (acetaminophen or ibuprofen) was reported by 86.8%.
- Comparator: No formal comparator or control group; this is a single-cohort descriptive study. Comparisons were internal across pain sources and by prior pain frequency.
- Follow-up: Followed up for 180 days via digital questionnaires; time-to-event outcomes (pain resolution, opioid discontinuation) censored within this period, with some surgical/low back estimates 'not reached'.
- Source basis: Based on the JAMA Network Open abstract, PubMed metadata (affiliations, MeSH), and Crossref bibliographic data. No PMCID/open full text was available.

#### Outcomes
- Primary: Median 20 days overall (IQR 8-88) among 1502 reporting pain; surgical pain 74 days (IQR 30 to not reached); low back pain 69 days (IQR 18 to not reached). (Median 20 days (IQR 8-88); surgical 74 days; low back 69 days.)
  - ⓘ Most patients' acute pain resolved within about 3 weeks, but surgical and low back pain commonly took over 2 months, so counsel these patients to expect a longer course.
  - 대부분의 급성 통증은 약 3주 내 해소되었으나 수술 및 요통은 흔히 2개월 이상 걸려, 이런 환자에게는 더 긴 경과를 예상하도록 안내해야 한다.
  - Stats rule: Median is the midpoint (half resolve sooner, half later); IQR shows the middle 50%. 'Not reached' means over half had not yet resolved by the follow-up limit, indicating a long or incomplete recovery.
  - 중앙값은 중간점(절반은 더 빨리, 절반은 더 늦게 해소); IQR은 가운데 50%를 보여준다. '미도달'은 추적 종료까지 절반 이상이 아직 해소되지 않았음을 의미하며 회복이 길거나 불완전함을 나타낸다.
- Secondary: Among 1189 (69.6%) reporting any opioid use, median time to discontinuation 7 days (IQR 2-31); an estimated 10.0% (95% CI, 7.7%-12.7%) used opioids >=90 days, higher in those reporting frequent pre-enrollment pain. (Median 7 days (IQR 2-31); 10.0% >=90 days (95% CI 7.7%-12.7%).)
  - ⓘ Typical opioid use was brief (about a week), yet roughly 1 in 10 continued for 90+ days, so identify higher-risk patients (e.g., frequent prior pain) for closer follow-up.
  - 전형적 오피오이드 사용은 짧았지만(약 1주) 약 10명 중 1명이 90일 이상 지속하여, 고위험 환자(예: 이전 잦은 통증)를 식별해 면밀히 추적해야 한다.
  - Stats rule: A 95% CI gives the plausible range for the true proportion; here 7.7%-12.7% means prolonged use is a real, non-trivial minority. It does not test against a threshold, just precision of the estimate.
  - 95% CI는 실제 비율의 타당한 범위를 제시하며, 여기서 7.7%-12.7%는 장기 사용이 실재하는 무시할 수 없는 소수임을 뜻한다. 특정 기준과 비교하는 검정이 아니라 추정치의 정밀도를 나타낸다.
- Secondary: Of 1482 completing >=1 survey in first 2 weeks, 77.8% used any opioids and 86.8% used acetaminophen/ibuprofen; median dose 10 MME/day (IQR 5-15); 66.9% (657/982) reported leftover opioids. (77.8% opioids, 86.8% nonopioids; median 10 MME/day (IQR 5-15); leftover 66.9%.)
  - ⓘ Multimodal analgesia was common and opioid doses were low, but two-thirds had leftover pills—reinforce nonopioid co-prescribing and prescribe smaller quantities with disposal counseling.
  - 다중모드 진통이 흔했고 오피오이드 용량은 낮았으나 3분의 2가 약이 남아—비오피오이드 병용 처방을 강화하고 더 적은 양을 처방하며 폐기 상담을 해야 한다.
  - Stats rule: These are simple proportions (percent of respondents). MME (morphine milligram equivalents) standardizes opioid dose across drugs; lower MME means lower opioid exposure.
  - 이들은 단순 비율(응답자 중 백분율)이다. MME(모르핀 밀리그램 등가)는 약물 간 오피오이드 용량을 표준화하며, MME가 낮을수록 오피오이드 노출이 적음을 의미한다.
- Takeaway: For opioid-naive adults with common acute pain, a short course (roughly a week or less) of low-dose opioids alongside acetaminophen/ibuprofen is consistent with observed real-world use; anticipate longer courses and closer follow-up for surgical and low back pain, and minimize excess pills to reduce leftover opioids.
- 임상 포인트: 흔한 급성 통증의 오피오이드 미경험 성인에서는 아세트아미노펜/이부프로펜과 함께 저용량 오피오이드를 단기간(약 1주 이하) 처방하는 것이 실제 사용 양상과 부합한다. 수술 및 요통은 더 긴 사용과 면밀한 추적을 예상하고, 남은 약을 줄이기 위해 과다 처방을 피해야 한다.
